Journalists

Directory.

The Journalists Directory identifies news media journalists who report on speech on college and university campuses in the United States.

To suggest a journalist to be added to the directory, please email lnott@freedomforum.org.

The Quad

A first-of-its-kind listing of organizations and influencers supporting free speech on college and university campuses in the United States and resource for research and survey data on campus speech issues.

The Quad was made possible by the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation and supported by the Charles Koch Foundation.
