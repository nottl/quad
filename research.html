Research

Submissions.

The Quad invites contributions from various academic disciplines exploring free expression issues on college campuses.

Selected papers, law review articles, research results, and other works of scholarship will be published on the Quad website.

Submissions are accepted on a rolling basis. Please send an abstract (no longer than 500 words) to lnott@freedomforum.org, with the words "Research Paper" in the subject line.
