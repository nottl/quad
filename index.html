A first-of-its-kind listing of organizations and influencers supporting free speech on college and university campuses in the United States and resource for research and survey data on campus speech issues.

The Quad is a campus free speech directory that identifies established groups, organizations, influencers, and news media journalists whose primary mission and responsibility are focused on championing free speech on college and university campuses in the United States. This is a non-partisan resource that lists entities all across the political spectrum. The Quad also aggregates survey and poll results relating to free speech on college campuses, and solicits curates research and scholarship pertaining to free speech on college campuses.

The directory is a project of the First Amendment Center of the Freedom Forum Institute, a neutral, non-partisan, non-profit organization that aims to educate Americans about how their First Amendment rights work, how they may be at risk, and how they can be protected. The research used to create the directory was conducted in partnership with the Fors Marsh Group (FMG).
